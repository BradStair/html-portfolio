The Best Movies according to Brad Stair

My Top 3 Movies of all time

1. Perks of Being a Wallflower

This movie makes me cry everytime I see it. It is beautifully acted and heartbreaking. I feel for this kid every time I watch the movie

2. Top Gun

This movie shaped my childhood in so many ways. I have loved this movie since I was 10 years old. Even though it is corny in places, I will forever watch this movie anytime it comes on the television.

3. Gladiator

Just a damn good movie
